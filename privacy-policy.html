Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 02, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KAVELD ("we," "us," "the operator") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in connection with KAVELD, a self-developed software tool used to manage products, inventory, and orders for our own Temu store through the Temu Open Platform API.

KAVELD is a self-use, internally operated application. It is not distributed to or used by other sellers, and it does not offer services to the public. It interacts only with our own Temu seller account and our own store's order data.

02Data We Collect

KAVELD retrieves the following categories of personal data from our Temu store via the Temu Open Platform API, solely to fulfill and manage customer orders:

Category Examples
Identity Customer name
Contact details Shipping/billing address, phone number, email address
Order information Order ID, items purchased, quantities, order status, shipment/tracking details
Product customization data Buyer-provided personalization details (where applicable to the ordered product)

We do not collect payment card details, passwords, or government ID numbers. Payments are processed entirely by Temu; we never receive or store payment credentials.

03How We Use Data

  • Processing, packing, and shipping customer orders
  • Synchronizing order status and tracking information back to Temu
  • Managing product listings and stock levels for our store
  • Resolving customer service inquiries related to an order
  • Complying with applicable tax, accounting, and commercial record-keeping obligations

We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to fulfilling and managing orders placed through our Temu store.

04Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the basis of: (a) performance of a contract (fulfilling the order the customer placed), and (b) compliance with legal obligations we are subject to (e.g. tax and commercial record retention).

05Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Personal data is shared only where necessary to fulfill an order:

  • Temu — as the marketplace through which the order was placed, and the source of the order data itself
  • Shipping carriers — limited to the recipient's name, address, and phone number, solely for delivery purposes

We do not use third-party cloud services, analytics providers, or logistics platforms beyond the shipping carrier(s) required to deliver an order.

06International Data Transfers

KAVELD runs on infrastructure located in Türkiye. Where personal data originating from customers in the European Economic Area, Switzerland, or the United Kingdom is processed outside those regions, we take appropriate steps to ensure the transfer complies with applicable data protection law, which may include relying on Standard Contractual Clauses or an equivalent recognized transfer mechanism.

This section should be confirmed against your actual setup — see the note below the document for why this matters.

07Data Retention

We retain order-related personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the order and to meet legal obligations, such as tax and commercial record-keeping requirements under applicable law. Data is deleted or anonymized once it is no longer required for these purposes.

08Security Measures

  • All communication with the Temu API is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Access to the application and the data it holds requires authentication
  • Only the operator has access to the data; there is no multi-user or third-party access

09Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below; we will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

10Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children, and we do not knowingly process personal data of children in connection with order fulfillment beyond what a parent or guardian provides as part of placing an order.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
